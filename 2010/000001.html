glad to hear that you like the program. You are the first user outside
my former institute of whom I'am aware of.

Concerning your first point, you found one of the several parts of the
program, where there is a button but no implementation behind it.
Saving of the cell is not possible at the moment.

Loading images should work out of the box. Images are handled either
by python and the PIL (tiff should work) or in the case of the Fuji

the image and I will have a look.

Concerning the future developement of clip (and the possible bugfixes)
I'am just working on a new version with improved speed and some new
features. So if you have any suggestions, please let me know.
